## **Formspree Setup (Contact Form)**

1. Go to https://formspree.io
2. Click "Create Project"
3. Create new form
4. Copy your Form ID
5. In `index.html`, find this line:
   ```html
   <form
     id="contactForm"
     action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID"
     method="POST"
   ></form>
   ```
6. Replace `YOUR_FORM_ID` with your actual ID
7. Save and redeploy
